Refund Policy

Every digital product on this site carries a 30-day money-back guarantee. If the files are not what you expected, you get your money back. All of it.

  • Effective · 2026-07-19
  • Window · 30 days from purchase
  • Digital · nothing to ship back

01The short version

Email us within 30 days of purchase and we refund you in full. No hostile questions, no hoops, no partial-credit games. The fastest route: reply directly to your order confirmation email — it already contains everything we need to find your order.

02How to ask

  • Fastest: reply to your order confirmation email. Your order details are right there in the thread.
  • Otherwise: email the support address shown on your receipt, with your order number if you have it.

A sentence about what went wrong helps us fix things — but it is never a condition of the refund. "It wasn't for me" is a complete answer.

03No physical returns

Everything we sell is a digital download. There is nothing to box up and ship back — no return label, no restocking fee, no RMA number. The refund closes the matter. We would rather you delete the files afterward, but there is no shipment and no inspection.

04When the money lands

We process refunds promptly — typically within a few business days of your email. After that, the timing is out of our hands: your bank or payment provider decides when the credit shows up. Realistically, that takes about 5–10 business days depending on the payment method; card refunds often post faster than bank transfers. If ten business days pass and nothing has arrived, write back and we will chase it from our side.

05What it covers

Every digital product sold through this site and the linked store — the build plans, the cut-file vault, the Commercial License, everything. The 30 days start on the day of purchase. One honest note: the guarantee exists so you can judge the files with the risk on us, not on you. Please do not treat it as a free rental — we are a small workshop, and the system runs on trust.

06Why we can offer this

Because the files are the product, and we know how they are made: every plan is drafted, dimensioned and checked by a human before it goes in the vault. The free sample plan shows that standard before you spend anything. The guarantee exists for the makers who try the files in good faith and find they are not a fit — everyone else never needs this page.
