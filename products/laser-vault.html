Questions

Before you buy

What exactly do I get?

One ZIP with 150+ curated SVG cut files, recommended settings per file, 3 bonus PDFs (profit pack, settings reference, pricing guide) and a design manifest.

What machines and software are the files compatible with?

SVG is the common tongue of laser work: it imports into any laser or vector software — LightBurn, Glowforge, xTool and similar. Settings are given as recommended starting points; always run a test square on your machine and material.

I'm a beginner — will this work for me?

Recommended settings remove the slowest part of learning. Honestly: you still need basic familiarity with your laser — focus, ventilation, material handling. Start with a single-layer ornament, then take on layered panels.

Can I sell what I make?

The vault is personal use by default. Add the Commercial License ($15) to sell finished pieces — or get it included with the Whole Workshop bundle. The profit pack and pricing guide cover the sell-side math.

What if it's not what I expected?
