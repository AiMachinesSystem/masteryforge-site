FAQ

Answers, before you buy

Seven questions cover almost everything we get asked. For the rest: the guarantee is one page, the refund is one email, and the sample plan is free.

What exactly do I get?

A ZIP per library, with the files in their native formats:

Metal Library — 500+ cut files in DXF + SVG across 8 categories, plus 2 starter build plans as PDFs (square fire pit, steel coffee table).
Wood Pattern Vault — 150+ scroll saw and fretwork patterns in SVG, plus 2 bonus PDF guides.
Laser Vault — 150+ curated SVG files with recommended settings, plus 3 bonus PDFs (profit pack, settings, pricing guide).
Clay Cutter Vault — 150+ earring cutter shapes in STL + SVG across 6 families, plus 3 bonus PDF guides.

Every library includes a design manifest, so you always know what you have and where it lives. The Whole Workshop Bundle adds the Commercial License.

How do I receive the files?

Instantly. A download link appears right after checkout, and the same link arrives by email. Re-downloading is free for life — including every future update to the libraries you own.

I'm a beginner — will this work for me?

Yes, with one honest condition. The libraries are built to be approachable even if this is your first build: the Metal Library starts with 2 beginner build plans with full cut lists and ordered steps, and the Laser Vault ships with recommended settings so you are not guessing at power and speed.

The condition: you need basic familiarity with your own tools. A plan can give you exact dimensions; it can't hold the welder for you.

What software and machines are the files compatible with?

DXF imports into plasma, laser and CNC CAD/CAM software. SVG opens in any vector editor or laser control software. STL loads in any 3D slicer. PDF opens in any reader.

These are open standards — if your machine's software imports DXF, SVG, STL or PDF, the files are ready for it.

Can I sell what I make?

Personal use is the default. To sell finished pieces, add the Commercial License — $15 on its own, or already included in the Whole Workshop Bundle. It is written in plain English and covers lifetime resale rights on finished pieces for one maker or shop of up to 2 people.

What if it's not what I expected?

Email us within 30 days of purchase and you get a full refund — no hostile questions, no hoops. It covers every product on the site, bundle included. The details are on the guarantee page.

Do the libraries get updated?

Yes. Every library receives lifetime updates. When new files are added to a library you own, re-download is free — you never pay twice for the same vault.
