FAQ

Answers, before you buy

Seven questions cover almost everything we get asked. For the rest: the guarantee is one page, the refund is one email, and the sample plan is free.

What exactly do I get?

A ZIP per product, with the files in their native formats:

Metalworking Project Plans — 500+ step-by-step build plans in PDF, plus 2 fully worked starter plans (square fire pit, steel coffee table) with cut lists and dimensions.
Metal Cut-File Vault — 500+ machine-ready cut files in DXF + SVG across 8 categories, for plasma, laser and CNC tables.
Commercial License — a one-page plain-English PDF that lets you sell the finished pieces you build.

Every library includes a design manifest, so you always know what you have and where it lives.

How do I receive the files?

Instantly. A download link appears right after checkout, and the same link arrives by email. Re-downloading is free for life — including every future update to the libraries you own.

I'm a beginner — will this work for me?

Yes, with one honest condition. The Metalworking Project Plans are built to be approachable even if this is your first build: the library starts with 2 beginner build plans with full cut lists and ordered steps.

The condition: you need basic familiarity with your own tools. A plan can give you exact dimensions; it can't hold the welder for you.

What software and machines are the files compatible with?

PDF opens in any reader and prints on any home printer — the build plans are dimensioned for hand tools. DXF imports into plasma, laser and CNC CAD/CAM software. SVG opens in any vector editor or laser control software.

These are open standards — if your machine's software imports DXF or SVG, the files are ready for it.

Can I sell what I make?

Personal use is the default. To sell finished pieces, add the Commercial License — $15, one-time. It is written in plain English and covers lifetime resale rights on finished pieces for one maker or shop of up to 2 people.

What if it's not what I expected?

Email us within 30 days of purchase and you get a full refund — no hostile questions, no hoops. It covers every product on the site. The details are on the guarantee page.

Do the libraries get updated?

Yes. Every library receives lifetime updates. When new files are added to a library you own, re-download is free — you never pay twice for the same vault.
