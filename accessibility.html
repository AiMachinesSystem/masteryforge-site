Accessibility statement

MasteryForge Crafts is committed to a website everyone can use. This page states our target, what we build with, and how to tell us when something blocks you.

01Our target

This site targets conformance with WCAG 2.2 Level AA. The European Accessibility Act (in force since June 28, 2025) applies to sellers serving EU consumers. We build to WCAG 2.2 AA and review this statement as the site evolves.

  • WCAG 2.2 AA
  • Last reviewed · July 20, 2026
  • Static site · no overlays

02What this site is built with

Accessibility is not a plugin here — it is the markup. The measures below are part of the site's code, and each one can be checked on any page:

  • Semantic HTML with one <h1> per page and a logical heading order.
  • A skip link as the first focusable element on every page.
  • Full keyboard operability with a visible focus ring that is never covered by sticky elements.
  • Text contrast of at least 4.5:1 and non-text contrast of at least 3:1, enforced through fixed design tokens that are never lightened.
  • Touch targets of at least 24px; primary buttons are 48px tall.
  • No horizontal scrolling at 320px width, and the layout stays usable at 200% zoom.
  • prefers-reduced-motion honored sitewide — every animation switches off when you ask for less motion.
  • Informative alt text on every product and build image; purely decorative blueprint drawings are hidden from assistive technology.
  • FAQs built on native <details> elements — the answers exist in the HTML and need no script to be read.
  • No autoplaying media anywhere: the build video plays only when you press play.
  • No cookies, no trackers, and no accessibility overlay widgets.

03Known limitations

The downloadable PDF plan sheets are technical drawings optimized for print; they are not yet fully tagged for screen readers. The build video has no spoken audio, so captions are not required; if narration is added, captions will ship with it.

04Feedback

Found a barrier? Tell us and we will fix it or work around it with you. Email us through the store's contact page — we aim to respond within 5 business days.

Open the contact page

05Evaluation method

The conformance claims in this statement are based on a self-assessment using automated and manual testing against WCAG 2.2 level AA. Testing covers text and non-text contrast, keyboard operability, focus visibility, reduced-motion behavior, zoom and reflow, and screen-reader markup.

06Enforcement

If you have reported a barrier and are not satisfied with our response, you may escalate the matter to the competent national authority that enforces Directive (EU) 2019/882 (the European Accessibility Act). In Italy, that authority is AgID — Agenzia per l'Italia Digitale.

For users in the United States, no equivalent formal enforcement channel applies to this site — but your feedback still helps us prioritize which fixes ship first.
