How to request a refund

The whole procedure

Three steps. One email is the hard part.

Step 01

Email us within 30 days

Thirty days from your purchase date is the only deadline. Reply to your order confirmation email — it reaches us directly — or write through the store's contact page.

Step 02

Tell us which order

The order email or order number is all we need to find your purchase. A reason helps us improve the next sheet, but you never owe us one.

Step 03

Get a full refund

The full purchase price, refunded. And because these are digital downloads, there is nothing to pack, print or ship back — the refund is the whole transaction.

No legal fog

The guarantee, in one paragraph

Buy any MasteryForge product and download everything. Open the files at your bench, measure twice, cut if you like. You have 30 days from the purchase date to decide whether the library earns its place in your workshop.

If it doesn't, one email gets you a full refund. We will not interrogate you, steer you through a form, or ask you to prove anything. Digital files can't be "returned" — so there is no return label, no package, no waiting for a warehouse to agree with you.

This page is the plain-English version. The formal policy text lives on our store's refund policy page — same rule, longer sentences.

What we will never do

No hostile questions. No "are you sure?" loop. No making you justify a refund to a form. The guarantee exists because the plans speak for themselves — and because a maker's trust is worth more than one sale.

We wrote this guarantee the way we would want to read it on someone else's site: one rule, a real deadline, and a human at the other end of the email.— THE MASTERYFORGE WORKSHOP
