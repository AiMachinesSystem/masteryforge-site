Why these plans work

A good plan behaves like a jig

It was never your hands. A bad file wastes good steel. These sheets are built so the measuring happens before the sparks do.

PILLAR 01

Every dimension checked

Each sheet is measured twice before it ships — against the drawing and against its own cut list. If a number could be misread at the bench, it gets redrawn until it can't. You measure your steel once, not your guesses twice.

PILLAR 02

Cut lists included

Every plan opens with a QTY / ITEM / SIZE cut list. Buy exactly what the project needs, cut every piece once, and lay the whole job out on the bench before the first tack. No mid-build supply run, no pile of offcuts you paid for.

PILLAR 03

Beginner-safe build sequence

Steps run in the order a build actually happens: cut, dry-fit, check square, then weld. First time striking an arc? The sheet tells you what to do next — and what not to do yet.
